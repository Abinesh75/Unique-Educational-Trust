ABOUT US..


VISION

Unique Educational Trust envisages a skilled and capable workforce that shares in, and contributes to, the benefits and opportunities of economic expansion and an inclusive growth path.

MISSION

Unique Educational Trust will increase access to high quality and relevant education and training and skills development opportunities including workplace learning and experience to enable effective participation in the economy and society by all Indians and reduce inequalities.

OVERVIEW


Unique Educational Trust believes in demonstrated competence, institutional integrity, personal commitment and deep sense of nationalism. A public charitable trust called “Unique Educational Trust” has been registered on 16/03/2007 under Indian Trust act 1882 with a view to increase access to high quality and relevant education and training and skills development opportunities including work place learning and experience, to enable effective participation in the economy and society by all Indians and reduce inequality. Adding to above, now the Unique Educational Trust feels to increase the livelihood of agricultural farmers in particular small farmers and marginal farmers. The trust deed was further amended on 01/03/2016 based on the resolution 04/2016 focusing the small farmers and marginal farmers in agriculture and stepped in to identify rural farmers and artisans and develop leadership, entrepreneurship and capacity building of the farmers both in technical and management and convert the potential skill of protecting agricultural business oriented end users in Tamil Nadu.

Empanelled as Resource Institution in Small Farmers Agri-Business Consortium (SFAC), New Delhi for promoting FPOs around India.

Unique Educational Trust (UET) is started for educational enhancement of needy people in earlier stage. Later turns its way in the agricultural field to help the farmers for the enhancement of their livelihood. For that UET gets empanelled in Small Farmers AgriBusiness Consortium (SFAC), New Delhi as a Resource Institution. Now SFAC promotes UET as the Resource Institution for the Eight States namely Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Telangana, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, Rajasthan. In Tamil Nadu, UET promotes 16 FPOs in Tirunelveli, Cuddalore, Theni, Sivagangai, Dharmapuri, Madurai, Tenkasi and Kanniyakumari districts.
